## Summary

Jenny Nguyen, a Vietnamese immigrant nail salon owner, has been a tenant for years paying rent on time. She wrote a heartfelt letter to ICI explaining economic hardship and requesting rent relief. ICI's response completely ignored her request and instead proposed rent INCREASES of 3-6% annually while her roof is actively leaking.

This demonstrates ICI's pattern of exploiting vulnerable tenants, particularly immigrants who may face language barriers and have fewer resources to fight back.
